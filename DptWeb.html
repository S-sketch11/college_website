About the Department

The Computer Science and Engineering Department is one of the most versatile departments of NTC and caters to the needs of other departments as well. The CSE department aims at developing the conceptual knowledge along with total personality, which helps students to face the challenges of rapidly changing IT industry. The program curriculum provides a strong foundation in all areas of computing and information technology with emphasis on practical learning. The Department has well experienced and dedicated faculty with different domain specific specializations. The department has initiated certain steps for path breaking and innovative research in various spheres. This research is being facilitated by support of the faculty, through projects, publishing books, articles and research papers on various topics.
